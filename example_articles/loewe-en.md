# The Lion - King of Animals

The lion is considered the king of animals and is one of the world's best-known big cats. Its majestic appearance and powerful roar make it unmistakable.

## Habitat

Lions inhabit the savannas and grasslands of Africa. In the past they were also found in parts of Asia and Europe; today, wild lions are found almost exclusively in sub-Saharan Africa.

## The pride

Unlike other big cats, lions are very social animals:
- A pride consists of 10-30 animals
- Several related females form the core
- 1-3 males protect the territory
- Shared rearing of the young

## Hunting behavior

Hunting is mainly the task of the females. They hunt mostly at dusk or at night and work closely together. Their main prey are:
- Zebras
- Wildebeest
- Antelopes
- Buffalo

## The mane

The males' magnificent mane has several functions:
- Protection in fights
- Sign of strength and health
- Attraction for females
- Intimidation of rivals

## Threats

Lions are classified as vulnerable. The main threats are:
- Habitat loss
- Conflicts with humans
- Lack of prey
- Illegal hunting

## Facts

- **Weight**: Males up to 250 kg, females up to 180 kg
- **Speed**: Up to 80 km/h over short distances
- **Roar**: Audible up to 8 km away
- **Life expectancy**: 10-14 years in the wild
